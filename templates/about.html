About This Project

This group project used multiple python and javascript libraries including Plotly, Chart.js, Leaflet, Flask, SQLAlchemy, Numpy, and Bootstrap in combination to create an interesting, interactive, and informative website that explores the relationship between player salaries and wins in the NBA. We also looked at money coming from sponsorships and the sectors of industry that are involved by looking at the arena sponsors for each team and breaking them into different business sectors. We hope that you find this information as interesting as we did. Thank you for visiting our website.
